"The Best Movies I Ever Watched"

My Top Three Movies List :

Titanic

Seventeen-year-old Rose hails from an aristocratic family and is set to be married.
When she boards the Titanic, she meets Jack Dawson, an artist, and falls in love with him.

Jab We Met

Aditya, a heartbroken business tycoon, aimlessly boards a train to escape his depressing life.
He meets Geet, a bubbly Punjabi girl, and gets pulled into her crazy life.

3 Idiots

In college, Farhan and Raju form a great bond with Rancho due to his refreshing outlook.
Years later, a bet gives them a chance to look for their long-lost friend whose existence seems rather elusive.
